TWomen get better at The Rose House. We believe that by engaging in high quality, gender specific, extended care addiction and mental health treatment, women can experience sustained sobriety and emotional healing. Our mission is to provide evidence-based, dual diagnosis treatment delivered by a compassionate staff within a dynamic community of recovery.

We focus on the whole person:

  • Psychological
  • Emotional
  • Physical
  • Spiritual
  • Nutritional
  • Cognitive
