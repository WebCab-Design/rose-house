Program Overview

An In-depth look at The Rose House.

The Rose House is an extended care, residential treatment program for women who are suffering from addiction/dual diagnosis. We explore and treat underlying issues that may have contributed to substance abuse, including, but not limited to, depression, anxiety, bi-polar disorder, abuse, and trauma.

We provide a psychological evaluation for each client upon admittance to customize individual treatment plans. Our holistic approach includes:

  • Intensive individual therapy
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy
  • Group therapy
  • Relapse prevention
  • Nightly outside AA meetings
  • MMPI-2 (Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Inventory)
  • Extensive fitness program
  • Nutritional counseling
  • Equine Therapy
  • Frequent group outings
  • Private location with exquisite views
  • Open space with hiking/biking trails
  • Minimum age 18
  • Handicapped Accessible
  • Program Length: 3 months plus
