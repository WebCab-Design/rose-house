Our women attend nightly outside 12 step meetings, are required to get a sponsor, and work the steps.  Twelve step work is not part of The Rose House clinical day and is accomplished in their free time.  Being of service is very important at The Rose House.  Not only do the women contribute to the 12 step community, they serve lunch to the homeless once a week, and volunteer at a local thrift store and food bank.
