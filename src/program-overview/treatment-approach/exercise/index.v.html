Physical fitness, yoga, and acupuncture are all proven to alleviate stress, pain, and cravings.  In addition to evidence-based therapies, The Rose House takes a holistic approach to wellness, offering an integrative model of care.  The women exercise daily at a beautiful athletic club that is designed for all fitness levels, weekly yoga is offered in house, and an acupuncturist trained in addiction is on site weekly.
