Marcie Chambersm P.hD

Director & The Rose House Founder

Dr. Marcie Chambers received her PhD in Psychology from the University of Colorado at Boulder. She was in private practice for a number of years, treating clients for depression, anxiety, trauma, codependency and addiction. Her practice included treating individuals, couples, and families. Dr. Chambers taught psychology at the University of Colorado, Regis University and Metro State College. Dr. Chambers opened The Rose House in Boulder County, Colorado in 2008. The Rose House is a 16 bed, extended care treatment facility for women. They focus on treating women with dual diagnosis including addiction, trauma, depression, anxiety, love addiction and codependency. The Rose House also has a sober living step down program that includes continued therapy. For more information about Dr. Chambers, please read Our Story.

Valerie Andrews, P.hD

Primary Therapist

Valerie Andrews, P.hD

Primary Therapist

Valerie L Andrews, PhD recently moved from New Zealand to Colorado in 2015. Her background and focus on deep therapy work and assessment led to her to wanting to work at Rose House. As a therapist here at the Rose House, Val works with the clients in trauma work, sex and love addiction, and CBT and DBT classes. She has been a volunteer at City Team Ministries and has worked as a Youth Coordinator on and off for 30 years. Since 2000, Val had a private practice in Los Gatos, CA with a focus on neuropsychology assessment and therapy. She was a professor for 10 years, teaching graduate students classes like Psychopathology, CBT, and Psychopharmacology. As a member Psi Chi, the International Honor Society for Psychology, Val received her Bachelor of Science in Psychology and Child Development at San Jose State University, earning honors and a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology and Neuropsychology at Pacific Graduate School in Palo Alto, CA.

   

Terri Kirwan, LPC

Primary Therapist

Terri Kirwan, LPC

Primary Therapist

Terri Kirwan is a Licensed Professional Counselor in the state of Colorado and has been practicing since 1998. She obtained her in BA in psychology from Naropa University and her MA in Counseling Psychology from Regis University. She has extensive training in 12 step and addiction recovery, body-centered psychotherapy, Somatic Experiencing and Sensorimotor Trauma Resolution work, Emotional Brain Training, DBT, CBT, Family Systems and couples therapy. She brings to The Rose House 18 years of experience working with addictions, trauma, eating disorders, mood disorders, personality disorders, and interpersonal challenges as well as running groups in DBT, CBT, Advanced Emotional Regulation and Interpersonal Skills, and Parenting. She has a great love of the outdoors where she actively pursues biking, skiing, hiking and canoeing.

Kathy Pahler

Community Outreach Coordinator | Yoga Instructor

Kathy was born and raised in North Carolina. She followed her brother to beautiful Boulder, CO in order to attend the University of Colorado. After receiving a BA in English, she pursued a career in sales and marketing. She discovered her passion for yoga and meditation after having children, and has been an avid practitioner and teacher for over twenty years. Being a part of The Rose House community appeals to her commitment to living a healthy and conscious lifestyle. She is excited to participate in the marketing of The Rose House, and feels that it is a wonderful place for women in recovery, integrating 12 step philosophy, creative therapeutic modalities, and experienced clinical support. In her free time, Kathy enjoys reading, biking, and cooking, as well as spending time with her husband and two adult children.
