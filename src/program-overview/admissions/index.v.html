Your recovery is important to us. We realize that committing to extended care treatment is a very important step. We would be happy to work with you to see if our treatment and your needs are a match. There are several ways to proceed.

Option 1: Call us to discuss your situation at 888-398-4111

Option 2: If you have insurance and would like us to check how your insurance would work with our program, click on “Verify Insurance”. After you securely email us brief information on your situation and your insurance information, we will be back in touch with you.

Option 3: If you'd like, please Click Here to leave a general contact inquiry and we will get back to you within 24 hours.

If we discover, together, that The Rose House is a fit, we will walk you through the admission process. This will include sending you a contract and determining an admission date and time.
