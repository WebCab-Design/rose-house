In Colorado, weather can change very quickly, so it is wise to dress in layers. Items you should bring upon admission to our alcohol and drug addiction treatment program:

  • Workout clothes
  • Exercise or gym shoes
  • Shoes or boots appropriate for equine therapy in various weather conditions (waterproof is best). Can be dusty/muddy!
  • Hiking Boots
  • Warm Jacket
  • Swim Suit
  • Sweatshirts
  • Pants and Jeans
  • Long sleeved and short sleeved shirts
  • Under garments
  • Modest/ casual attire appropriate for long-term stay
  • Toothbrush
  • Toothpaste (No alcohol in the first three ingredients). Sealed and brand new ONLY
  • Shampoo
  • Conditioner (No alcohol in the first 3 ingredients) – Sealed and brand new ONLY
  • Face cleanser (No alcohol in the first 3 ingredients) – Sealed and brand new ONLY
  • Lotion (No alcohol in the first 3 ingredients) – Sealed and brand new ONLY
  • Hairdryer
  • All prescribed medication in their appropriate bottle
  • Personal Photos
    • Optional Items
      • Some initial spending money (until your spending account is established)
      • Craft Supplies
      • Musical Instrument
      • Extra Socks
      • Music device that does not connect to the internet
      • Headphones
      • Recovery Appropriate Books
      • Attire for graduations/celebrations
      • Water Bottle
